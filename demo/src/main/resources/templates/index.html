About IMA-PG India

IMA-PG India Private Limited has been able to carve a niche in the Indian Pharma Machinery industry by focusing on engineering excellence and being customer centric in its approach. Collaboration of PG with IMA SpA, was very strategic and brought about a new wave of diverse activities in its product offerings to the pharma machinery and packaging industry. This paved path for the new era of machines with emphasis on automation complimenting the existing range.
